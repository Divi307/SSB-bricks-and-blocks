Fly Ash Bricks

Strong and versatile concrete blocks for various applications.

  • Brick Dimension: 190 × 95 × 95 mm

AAC Block Bricks

Durable and reliable bricks for all your construction needs.

  • Strength: 3 N/mm² (can go up to 4.5–5 N/mm²)
  • Water Absorption: 10% by weight (as per IS 2185 Part 3)
  • Available Dimensions:
    600 × 200 × 100 mm
    600 x 200 x 150 mm
    600 x 200 x 200 mm
    600 x 200 x 250 mm
  • Weight approx: 7-18kg (varying with dimensions)
